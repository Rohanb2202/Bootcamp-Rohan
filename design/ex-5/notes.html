Exercise 5: Layout Principles

Level 1

  • Recreate the layout of a simple webpage (e.g., medium.com article).
  • Focus on:
    • Applying consistent margins and padding.
    • Using white space to create clear separation between sections.
    • Aligning text and images correctly.

Level 2

  • Demonstrate a layout that emphasizes visual hierarchy.
    • Design a homepage with a large hero section, a featured article grid, and a footer.
    • Use larger fonts and bolder colors for important elements.
    • Add gutters between sections to maintain visual clarity.
    • Apply a CSS grid system to organize content effectively.

My Approach

Level 1

In Level 1, I recreated the layout of a basic webpage by focusing on consistent margins and padding. White space was strategically used to create clear separation between sections. I ensured proper alignment of text and images to enhance readability and maintain a clean design.

Level 2

For Level 2, I designed a homepage emphasizing visual hierarchy by including a large hero section, a featured article grid, and a footer. Larger fonts and bolder colors were applied to highlight important elements. Gutters were added between sections to improve visual clarity, and a CSS grid system was used to effectively organize the content.
