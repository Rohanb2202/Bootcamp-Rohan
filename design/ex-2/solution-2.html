The Road to Enlightenment

Littering a dark and dreary road lay the past relics of browser-specific tags, incompatible DOMs, broken CSS support, and abandoned browsers.

We must clear the mind of the past. Web enlightenment has been achieved thanks to the tireless efforts of folk like the W3C, WaSP, and the major browser creators.

The CSS Zen Garden invites you to relax and meditate on the important lessons of the masters. Begin to see with clarity. Learn to use the time-honored techniques in new and invigorating fashion. Become one with the web.

The Power of CSS

CSS provides complete control over a document's visual presentation. The Zen Garden demonstrates how the same HTML can be transformed dramatically through creative styling.

Your Design Journey

Modify the CSS freely, exploring responsive design, typography, and visual storytelling. Use the sample files as a starting point for your creative exploration.

Why Participate?

Gain recognition, inspiration, and contribute to a living gallery of web design techniques. Learn from others and push the boundaries of what's possible with CSS.

Design Guidelines

Focus on CSS 1 & 2 with careful CSS 3 & 4 implementation. Ensure cross-browser compatibility and validate your CSS.
